HMRC Design Patterns backlog

Anyone can propose, develop or contribute to HMRC Design Patterns. You can propose a new pattern, or suggest an improvement to an existing one.

These are patterns designed specifically for the needs of HMRC users. If you want to propose a new pattern for use across government, or suggest an improvement to an existing one, use the GOV.UK Design System community backlog.

Patterns in the backlog may be:

  • Proposed – someone has suggested a new pattern
  • To do – the proposed pattern has been agreed and is ready to work on
  • In progress – someone is actively working on the pattern
