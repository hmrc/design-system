Account menu

development

This is currently development because more research is needed.

The account menu is used in, and was designed for, the personal tax account.

When to use

If your service is part of the personal tax account.

When not to use

If your service is not part of the personal tax account. Do not use the account menu.

How it works

The account menu contains:

  • an account home link
  • a messages link with a notification badge
  • a check progress link
  • a menu to manage account settings
  • a sign-out link
