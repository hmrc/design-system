    Exchange rates: yearly averages and spot rates

    Document type

    Publication guidance - stub page and CSV attachment.

    Collection type

    Add the page to the Rates and Allowances: HM Revenue and Customs collection page.

    How it works

    Stub page

    Title

    Enter this text:

      HMRC foreign exchange rates: yearly averages and spot rates

    Summary

    Enter this text:

      Find current and historical foreign rates of exchange

    Details

    Enter this text:

      The average yearly exchange rates data is based on figures published in the Financial Times

    CSV attachment

    Title

    Enter the month or period the rates apply to.

    For the yearly averages:

      Average for the year to [date]

    For example, ‘Average for the year to 31 March 2017’.

    For spot rates:

      Spot rates on [date] and [date]

    For example, ‘Spot rates on 31 December 2016 and 31 March 2017’.

    Column headings

    Use these column headings

        Currency units per £1
